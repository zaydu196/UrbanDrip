Who We Are

UrbanDrip Clothing is a modern streetwear brand created for young people who want to stay stylish without spending too much money. We are not just a clothing brand — we are a movement built on self-expression, street culture, and the belief that fashion should be for everyone.

Our collection includes a carefully curated range of hoodies, t-shirts, bottoms, footwear and accessories. Every item is designed to be simple, comfortable and suitable for everyday wear, whether you are heading to class, linking with friends or just moving through the city.

UrbanDrip aims to build a strong connection with its audience through fashion and social media. The brand is inspired by current street culture and the popular trends shaping the lives of teenagers and young adults across South Africa and beyond.

Our Values

Affordability

We believe everyone deserves to look good without breaking the bank. Our prices are kept low so more people can enjoy quality streetwear — fashion should never be a privilege reserved for the few.

Style

We keep up with the latest trends so our customers always have access to fresh, modern looks. From classic black hoodies to bold graphic tees, UrbanDrip keeps you one step ahead.

Community

UrbanDrip is more than a clothing brand. We are building a community of young, creative people who express themselves through fashion, music and culture. When you wear UrbanDrip, you represent something bigger than just a label.

Authenticity

Everything we create is rooted in real street culture. We listen to our community, respond to trends as they happen, and stay true to the identity that built us.

Our Story

UrbanDrip started with a simple idea — make streetwear accessible to everyone. Founded in 2024, we began by selling a small range of hoodies and t-shirts online. The response was overwhelming. Within weeks, orders were coming in from across the country and we realised we had tapped into something real.

We quickly expanded our range to include footwear, bottoms and accessories. Every product added to the catalogue is checked for quality and style, because we refuse to compromise on either. Growth has been fast, but our values have stayed the same from day one.

Today we continue to grow, adding new items every month and connecting with customers across South Africa and beyond. The dream is simple: to be the go-to streetwear brand for the next generation — and we are just getting started.
